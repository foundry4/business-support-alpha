Find support options for your business

Before applying for additional funding, you should consider:

  • How you will spend the money
  • How soon you need the money
  • How upfront costs, such as employing more staff or setting up new premises, affect your cash flow
  • What assets – like a car or a house – you will be able to use as a guarantee (if you’re a sole trader)

Talking to a professional adviser can help you decide whether it makes financial sense to take on loans or investment. You can find a chartered accountant on the Institute of Chartered Accountants (ICAEW) website, or a solicitor on the Law Society website.
