Early Stage

When you’re at the beginning of your business journey, there’s a lot to think about. From setting up operations legally and hiring the right people to drawing up a sensible business plan, it’s easy for things to slip your mind

At this stage, help is available in many forms, with government-funded services on hand to give you the best possible chance of success.

We think you’ll be interested in the following services:

  • Financial support from Start Up Loans
    The Start Up Loans scheme is backed by the government. It offers free mentoring and low-interest personal loans of up to £25,000.
    Find out more

  • Advice straight to your inbox from Enterprise Nation
    Sign up for an email-only seven-day course, filled with practical advice and tips for starting your business.
    Find out more
