Because your business is growing rapidly and has a turnover greater than £200k, you could benefit from services that support growing, scaling and expanding.

Growth is great for your business but it can create a lot of pressure. Your business needs to stay balanced in order to sustain any growth: you can’t serve ten times as many customers with the same number of products.

During this phase, financial support may be what your business needs most. Other options include focusing on innovation and beginning to work with the public sector.

We think you’ll be interested in the following services:

  • Finance support from the British Business Bank
    Equity crowdfunding or angel investor? Invoice financing or sector-specific grant? Use the Finance Hub to learn about finance options and understand which is best for your business.
    Find out more

  • Public sector contract advice from AXA
    The government is committed to awarding public sector contracts to small businesses, and AXA has guidance on how to research, price and write winning bids.
    Find out more

  • Funding and loans from Innovate UK
    Innovate UK is a government-backed agency that supports businesses with loans and funding worth up to £10 million. Read a detailed overview of how to apply.
    Find out more

  • Mentorship programmes from Be the Business
    A fast-growing business needs a proven mentor, and Be the Business works with companies such as McKinsey & Company, Amazon and EY. Benefit from a mentor’s experience, network and contacts.
    Find out more
