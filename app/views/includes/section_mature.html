Once you’ve been operating for a while, it becomes easy to stick to the processes you know and trust to produce results. But the outside world is always changing, and the success of your business depends on your ability to change with it.

Perhaps your productivity could be better, achieving more with the same resources. It might be time to tap into a new market, or take on apprentices to bring fresh talent into the workforce. Whatever boost you choose, there are government-backed schemes to support you.

We think you’ll be interested in the following services:

  • Productivity benchmarking from Be the Business
    Productivity directly affects the performance of your business. See how your productivity compares with similar companies, and learn how to improve it.
    Find out more

  • Exporting opportunities at great.gov.uk
    Overseas buyers are looking for products and services from the UK. Use this tool to find the right export opportunities for your business.
    Find out more

  • Online masterclasses with Enterprise Nation
    Subject-matter experts and successful entrepreneurs share their advice on topics such as upskilling staff and increasing sales. Browse a large library of online masterclasses.
