{{ location.LEP }} Growth hub

For pre-start, start-up and established businesses

{{ location.LEP }} Growth Hub & Skills Hub helps eligible businesses to identify and access the right support. The Growth Hub focuses on services for very early stage businesses, while the Skills Hub supports established companies with five or more employees.

Services include:

  • Access to and entry into a local business directory
  • Access to guidance on regulation and environmental sustainability
  • Finding training to upskill or expand a team and improve productivity (Skills Hub)
  • Finding affordable training, workshop and recruitment providers (Skills Connectors)
  • A business skills review and personalised template for HR processes and policies (Skills Toolkit)
